# Images Folder

Place your 5 images here with these names:

- `photo1.jpg` (or .png, .webp)
- `photo2.jpg`
- `photo3.jpg`
- `photo4.jpg`
- `photo5.jpg`

## Tips:

- **Recommended size:** 800x600 pixels or similar 4:3 aspect ratio
- **Format:** JPG for photos, PNG for graphics, WEBP for best compression
- **File size:** Keep under 1-2MB each
- **Naming:** Must match the names in `hooks/useImageOrder.ts`

## Where to Get Sample Images:

If you don't have your own images yet, you can:
1. Use your own photos
2. Download free stock photos from:
   - [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com)
   - [Pexels](https://pexels.com)
   - [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com)
